1) Typography: text, spacing, fonts

“Typography is the craft of endowing human language with a visual form.” — Robert Bringhurst

Google Fonts: add a <link> before your CSS or an @import at the top of your stylesheet, then set font-family.
Example: <link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Inter:wght@400;600;700&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
