Chapter 6 Images

Images & Figure

Core attributes (src, alt, title, width, height, loading) and <figure>/<figcaption> — with live previews.

<img> attributes

  • src: absolute or relative URL to the image file.
  • alt: alternative text for accessibility / fallback.
  • title: optional tooltip on hover; less important than alt.
  • width & height: reserve space to prevent CLS (use the file’s intrinsic dimensions).
  • loading: eager (default) or lazy (load near viewport). 
<!-- Figure wrapping an image and its caption -->
<figure>
  <img
    src="../../assets/html-6.png"
    alt="Working Cat"
    title="Learn adding image"
    width="1271"   <!-- replace with your image's intrinsic width -->
    height="1271"  <!-- replace with your image's intrinsic height -->
    loading="eager"
  >
  <figcaption>Hello — this is the demo image.</figcaption>
</figure>

<figure> variants

<figure>
  <figcaption>Caption can come before the content</figcaption>
  <p><code>&lt;h1&gt;Hello World!&lt;/h1&gt;</code></p>
</figure>
